<h1>Simp Quiz</h1>

<p>Simple Front-End project that makes a quiz to figure out if the user is a SIMP or not.</p>
<p>A SIMP is someone who idolizes their lover, worshipping them and sacrificing their own welfare for their behalf.</p>
<p>Complete this Simple Quiz and find out your SIMP status.</p>
<p>Have Fun with this Game.</p>
